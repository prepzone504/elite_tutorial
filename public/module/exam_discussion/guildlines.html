Discussion Guidelines

Please review the rules below before entering the live chat room.

  • ⏱️
    Fastest Fingers First You have 30 seconds to vote (A-D). The round ends early if 5 people answer correctly.
  • 🔒
    Lock Your Vote Votes cannot be changed once confirmed. Your selection remains hidden from others.
  • 👑
    Top Scorer Speed matters! The fastest correct answer gets crowned Top Scorer for the round.
  • 🎙️
    Open Discussion After the answer is revealed, a 1-minute open chat begins. You can text or send voice notes.
  • 🤝
    Respect & Focus Be polite and keep the discussion strictly focused on the exam topic.
